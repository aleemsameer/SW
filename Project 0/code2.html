MSIT

____More Information about Msit______

MSIT (MS in IT or MS in Information Technology) is a two-year postgraduate program in computer science which began in 2001. MSIT is an ambitious postgraduate interdisciplinary multi-university programme. The MSIT program is being offered by the Universities created "Consortium of Higher Learning Institutions" (CIHL).Several Carnegie Mellon Researchers are getting feedback on the contents of the course under MOU with CMU. Actually, MSIT is offered at the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT-H), Hyderabad, IT School, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH), Engineering College, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK), Engineering College, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological,University Anantapur (JNTUA) and Sri Venkateswara University Tirupati (SVU).


MSIT was developed to develop professionals who are technologically capable, highly competitive and self-motivated in accordance with the demands of an ever-changing market. The program aims to train not only IT professionals but also experts from other fields providing a special and rigorous curriculum that introduces the students to some of India's finest universities as well as abroad. They will be armed at the conclusion of their stay with MSIT as they are with freshly acquired faith and vigor, move out into the world and continue to practice the skills they have gained in this programme.
